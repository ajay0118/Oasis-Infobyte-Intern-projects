🌟 Tribute to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam 🌟

"The Missile Man of India & People’s President"

About

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam (1931–2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a pivotal role in India’s civilian space program and military missile development. Known as the "Missile Man of India," he inspired millions with his vision for a self-reliant and developed nation.

Major Achievements

  • Key role in India’s Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Contributed to ISRO’s SLV-III, India’s first satellite launch vehicle.
  • Honored with the Bharat Ratna in 1997.
  • Authored inspirational books like Wings of Fire and Ignited Minds.
  • Served as the 11th President of India (2002–2007).
